Bonita's Page

 

 

Bonita graced the Mena clan with her presence in May of 2001. We rescued her from the clutches of the Bridgeport Animal Shelter in Connecticut via Petfinder.org. She is the proud owner of Mary and Paul. As far as we can tell, Bonita is part Australian Cattle Dog and part American Staffordshire Terrier but all dingo.
