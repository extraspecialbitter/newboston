Rekooperators Return to Harpers Ferry

 

The Rekooperators, featuring legendary multi-instrumentalist Al Kooper, guitarist Jimmy Vivino, bassist Mike Merritt (both of the "Conan O'Brien Show") and drummer Anton Fig (from "The Late Show with David Letterman"), take the stage at 7:30 pm on Saturday April 25th in a triumphant return to the Allston club. Special Guests the Love Dogs play their unique brand of jump blues at 10:30 pm. Steve Morse of the Boston Globe wrote: "The Rekooperators... played urban and country blues, jazz and soul with a polished yet instinctual feel that comes from mutual respect and decades of experience". Tickets are $14 in advance (at the club only) or $16 at the door (if available).

 

Tickets are available exclusively at the club - $14 in advance and $16 the day of the show (if they don't sell out first).
