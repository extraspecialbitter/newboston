Harpers Ferry provides a casual, friendly atmosphere for listening to favorite local, regional and national blues and R&B acts. Harpers boasts a fully-equipped kitchen with everything from pizza to buffalo wings. We now offer full-service catering as well.

Harpers has won Boston Magazine's Best Blues Club Award 3 times and has been named Boston's Best Blues Club by Rolling Stone Magazine and the Boston Globe.
