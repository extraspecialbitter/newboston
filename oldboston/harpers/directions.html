WARNING: at this point Commonwealth Ave turns sharply to the left...(so stay to the right). If you continue straight you are on Brighton Ave.

Follow Brighton Ave. 'til you pass Harvard Ave intersection and Harper's Ferry is on your immediate left.

PARKING

The parking lot directly behind Harpers Ferry is divided into two sections: the section closest to the club is clearly marked as being reserved for customers of Blanchard's Liquor Store. DO NOT PARK IN THIS SECTION! Your car WILL be towed! The remainder of the lot is dedicated to free public parking and is available for Harpers Ferry guests. Enter the lot from the western side of Harvard Street, just south of Brighton Avenue. And do try to come out early - the lot fills up fast! And when this happens, do NOT park in the Osco Drug parking lot or on the residential side streets (which are clearly designated for permit parking only). With a little bit of patience (and a little more parallel parking expertise) you can eventually find a spot on Harvard Street or Brighton Avenue.
