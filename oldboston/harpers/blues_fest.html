Harpers Ferry 12th Annual Blues Festival

 

This year's Boston Blues Fest features most every shade of blue....from the irresistibly danceable Louisiana sounds of Buckwheat Zydeco to the rockin' blues of Al Kooper and Buddy Miles to the classic sounds of Bo Diddley and Luther "Guitar Jr." Johnson. The festival hits the home stretch with two dynamic blues ladies... Debbie Davies and Susan Tedeschi. Don't miss a minute of it!
