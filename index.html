New Boston Promotions
Your Source For Imprinted Promotional Products


New Boston Promotions was founded by Mary Melodee Mena in 1988 to provide promotional products for clients of all sizes. Mary will help you use promotional products to deliver your message to your target audience without waste. Our products motivate, promote, reward and reinforce your message — and they are desired and appreciated when received. Most of all, your message is kept and remembered  — unlike traditional advertising.

Why New Boston Promotions?

  • I have access to over 500,000 promotional items — all available with your logo on them — and the experience to find the best value for your defined need.
  • I can use my 25 years in the promotional products industry to navigate through a vast number of suppliers and make a recommendation that will achieve your goals.
  • I can offer advice about artwork and graphics, screening, embossing, engraving and dozens of other production methods and how to put them together for you.
  • I am your dedicated creative resource. My priority is to ensure that you make the greatest impact within your available budget.
  • I will follow up to make sure the products get delivered when and where you need them.
  • I am affiliated with Geiger, the largest family-owned and managed promotional products company in the world.
