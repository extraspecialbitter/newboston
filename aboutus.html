About Us

In 1988 New Boston Promotions began as a small promotional products distributorship. In 1992 we formed a strategic alliance with Geiger, the largest family-owned and managed company in the promotional products industry. The combination of the two provides our clients with the flexibility, creativity and personal service of a small distributorship coupled with the competitive pricing, stability and technical know-how of an industry giant. Geiger is a fourth-generation company, with 500 full-time employees and in 2006 it reported revenues of over 146 million dollars.

The past 20 years have been exciting ones for us and our clients. We have not only provided clients with "basic" promotional products such as pens, mugs, wearables and mousepads but we have worked on some cutting-edge projects that included individually shipping live bonsai trees in imprinted pots, creating custom juggling toys, providing logoed remote control cars and using our overseas sourcing to produce custom shaped stress toys.

Our products have been mentioned in Rolling Stone Magazine, purchased by a U.S. president, worn by rock stars and have traveled to the bottom of the ocean. Just think what we can do for you!

The canine supermodel used on our website and mousepad is not just another pretty face. “Bonita” became a part of New Boston Promotions in May of 2001. She was rescued from an inner-city dog pound in Connecticut only days before she was scheduled to be put down. She is an over-achieving Australian Cattle Dog/American Staffordshire Terrier mix. When not doing mousepad photo shoots other corporate duties include acting as an early warning device for UPS deliveries, procuring treats and power napping on the office floor.
